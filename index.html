FileSeek Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24.05.2026

FileSeek is a privacy-first file search and management app for Android. This policy explains how FileSeek handles your information. The short version: it doesn't collect any.

Data we collect

None. FileSeek does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. We have no accounts, no servers, and no analytics.

How your files are handled

All file scanning, search indexing, thumbnails, and the encrypted vault are processed entirely on your device. Your files, file names, search queries, and index never leave your phone. We cannot see them, and neither can anyone else.

Network use

FileSeek connects to the network for one purpose only: processing a one-time Pro purchase through Google Play Billing. This transaction is handled by Google; FileSeek does not receive or store your payment details. The app contains no advertising and no third-party tracking or analytics software.

The encrypted vault

Files you place in the vault are encrypted on your device using industry-standard cryptography. The encryption keys are derived from your passphrase and stored in your device's secure hardware. Because there is no backdoor and nothing is sent to us, if you lose both your passphrase and your recovery phrase, the encrypted files cannot be recovered by anyone, including us.

Permissions

FileSeek requests broad file access (All files access) because it is a file manager and search tool — it needs to see your files to let you search and manage them. This access is used solely on-device for that purpose.

Children's privacy

FileSeek does not collect data from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new "last updated" date.
